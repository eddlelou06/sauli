Sauli


BROWNIES.
Process

1. Preheat the oven to 350F or 180C.

2. Prepare a square pan (20x20 centimeters). Put aluminium foil inside.

3.In a glass bowl add the butter and the cocoa, melt in the microwave on high power 45 seconds. Mix with a fork, add an egg and mix until it's incorporate, do the same thing with the second egg. Add the flour and baking powder, mix until it's incorporate

4. Pour on the square pan.

5. Bake for 20-25 minutes.

6. Let cool before unmold and cut.
